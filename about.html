About us

You are probably not here to here to whole long story about how this business had its birth, so I will tell you the short version. Tim and Annie are both passionate hikers and campers, and that is even how they met 10 years ago. Before they got married and had children they enjoyed many adventures together and on these journeys there was always something missing. A decent rain jacket that would keep them comfortable and warm for the entire journey that also had the right quality and design at a reasonable price. When they got their first child they could no longer go on as many adventures anymore and the idea of creating and designing their own line of jackets grew. They are passionate about the production and design, and are very proud of the fact that their jackets are also eco-friendly.
